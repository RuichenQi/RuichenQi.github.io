About Me

Ruichen Qi received the B.S. degree in Integrated Circuit & Integrated System from Shandong University, Jinan, Shandong Province, China, in 2023, and he is currently a first-year master student in the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, USA majoring in Very Large Scale Integration Circuit(VLSI). His current research interests include microarchitecture, low-power and high-performance VLSI design and systems. [View my resume](https://ruichenqi.github.io/assets/resume/Resume.pdf)
