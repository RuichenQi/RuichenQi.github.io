About Me

I received the B.S. degree in Integrated Circuit & Integrated System from Shandong University, Jinan, Shandong Province, China, in 2023, and I am currently a first-year master student in the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI, USA majoring in Very Large Scale Integration Circuit(VLSI). My current research interests include microarchitecture, low-power and high-performance VLSI design and systems.
